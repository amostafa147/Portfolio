UCSD DigiHealth Lab

Undergraduate Researcher (April 2021 - Present)

About

×

I am working as an undergraduate researcher under the guidance of Professor Edward Wang at UCSD's DigiHealth Lab, a joint research laboratory of UCSD Design Lab and the Jacobs School of Engineering ECE department. I am working with undergraduate and PhD students to develop a wearable health device that measures certain levels of the wearer, such as blood sugar. My contributions include writing Arduino/C++ code for I2C communication, designing circuits on Autodesk Eagle, soldering them, and more.
